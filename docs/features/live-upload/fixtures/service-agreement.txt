MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT
Between: Aletheia Logistics LLC ("Provider")
And: the Customer identified in the applicable Order Form ("Customer")
Effective Date: 1 January 2026   ·   Agreement No. MSA-2026-0007

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") governs the provision of logistics
and fulfillment services by Provider to Customer. Defined terms appear in Section 1.
Payment and suspension terms appear in Section 4.

📑 PAGE 2 — SECTION 1: DEFINITIONS

1.1  "Invoice" means a written request for payment issued by Provider for Services
     rendered in a billing period.
1.2  "Due Date" means the date, stated on each Invoice, by which payment is due.
     Unless an Order Form states otherwise, the Due Date is thirty (30) days after
     the Invoice date (Net 30).
1.3  "Overdue" means any Invoice that remains unpaid in whole or in part after its
     Due Date has passed.
1.4  "Suspension" means the temporary discontinuation of Services under Section 4.

📑 PAGE 3 — SECTION 4: PAYMENT, OVERDUE INVOICES, AND SERVICE SUSPENSION

4.1  Payment. Customer shall pay each undisputed Invoice in full by its Due Date.
4.2  Late Interest. Overdue amounts accrue interest at 1.5% per month from the Due
     Date until paid.
4.3  SERVICE SUSPENSION. Provider may suspend Services to Customer if any undisputed
     Invoice remains overdue by more than thirty (30) days. Provider will give
     Customer at least seven (7) days' written notice before suspending, during which
     Customer may cure the overdue amount to avoid suspension. Suspension does not
     relieve Customer of the obligation to pay overdue amounts.
4.4  Reinstatement. Provider will restore Services within two (2) business days after
     Customer has paid all overdue amounts and any accrued late interest.
4.5  No Waiver. Provider's decision not to suspend on any occasion is not a waiver of
     its right to suspend on a later occasion.

📑 PAGE 4 — SECTION 9: GENERAL

9.1  Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Delaware.
9.2  Entire Agreement. This Agreement and the Order Forms are the entire agreement
     between the parties and supersede all prior discussions.
9.3  Notices. Notices under Section 4.3 must be in writing to the address on the
     applicable Order Form.
