# Job: AI Business Assistant (AI-Powered Business Knowledge Assistant)

- **Category:** General Virtual Assistance
- **Posted:** Jun 7, 2026
- **Role sought:** Senior AI engineer to build an MVP

## Summary

Build an MVP of an **AI-powered Business Knowledge Assistant**.

> This is **NOT** a PDF chatbot.

The goal is a system that answers business questions by **retrieving and combining
information from multiple sources**, while maintaining **traceability and source
attribution**.

## MVP Data Sources

- PDF documents
- Structured SQLite data

## Required Capabilities

The system should be able to:

- Accept free-form natural language questions
- Decide which source(s) are relevant (query routing)
- Retrieve information from documents, databases, or both
- Combine information into a grounded answer
- Provide citations and traceability
- Support **English and Hebrew**

## Example Questions

1. "What contracts expire in the next 90 days and what penalties are defined in
   those contracts?"
2. "Which customers have overdue payments and what does the agreement say about
   service suspension?"
3. "Show all active projects and summarize the risks mentioned in their
   documentation."

## Future Integrations (not in MVP, but architecture must account for them)

- CRM systems
- Email systems
- Cloud storage
- Case management systems
- Hybrid local/cloud environments

## What the Client Does NOT Want

> "I am NOT looking for someone who simply uploads PDFs into a vector database
> and performs semantic search."

## Specifically Looking For Experience With

- Query routing
- Hybrid retrieval
- RAG + SQL systems
- Grounded generation
- Source attribution
- Extensible architecture

## Application Requirements

MUST include at least one of: a **GitHub repository**, **architecture diagram**,
**Loom video**, **live demo**, or **product** that demonstrates work similar to
what is described above.
